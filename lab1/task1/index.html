Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas eat bamboo for 99% of their diet.
  • They can spend up to 14 hours a day eating.
  • Pandas are excellent tree climbers and swimmers.
  • They are native to the mountain ranges of central China.
  • Pandas are a symbol of peace and conservation worldwide.
